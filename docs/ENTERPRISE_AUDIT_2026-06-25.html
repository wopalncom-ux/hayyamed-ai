1 · Executive Summary

A multi-disciplinary inspection (frontend, backend/security, AI/automation) was run against the actual codebase and the live production system. The platform is a genuinely functional multi-tenant CRM with a real workflow engine, RAG knowledge base, multi-provider AI router, and 26 backend modules — not a mockup. However, it is not yet enterprise-production-ready: this audit found and fixed a critical cross-tenant security hole, a blocking page crash, and two master-admin 500 errors, all verified resolved in production this session.

The headline gap versus the stated vision ("AI Business Operating System, owner-controlled, AI-provider-flexible") is that several capabilities are modeled in the database but not wired to execution — most notably per-agent/per-tool AI provider config and RAG context injection. These are build items, not bugs, and are itemised below.

3 · Master Owner Control Panel

Owner account: wopalncom@gmail.com · password Owner@2025 (change on first login) · role SUPER_ADMIN.

Now has verified full access to all platform-admin surfaces: stats, all orgs, audit logs, customer health, AI quality, AI observability, feature flags, marketplace admin. The OwnerGuard recognises SUPER_ADMIN role or an owner-email allowlist (extendable via PLATFORM_OWNER_EMAILS env). No non-owner user can reach these — confirmed 403.

Gap The existing assertOwner still also grants AGENCY_ADMIN platform access (a customer persona). Recommend tightening to SUPER_ADMIN-only and scoping agency admins to their own sub-accounts. Not changed yet to avoid breaking the live agency feature.

9 · What "100%" Requires

To reach a client-presentable, enterprise-ready 100%:

  • Security 100: credential encryption, RLS or audited isolation, all P0 items closed.
  • AI OS 100: provider control center live, agents+RAG executing, cost caps enforced, file upload working.
  • Integrations 100: at least WhatsApp + Stripe + one calendar actually transacting, with test-connection + logs.
  • UX 100: responsive across phone/tablet/desktop; Lighthouse 95-100; consistent loading/empty/error states.
  • Automation 100: templates, approval flows, handover, end-to-end tested.
  • Ops 100: Gemini/Groq keys in Secret Manager, Postmark live, monitoring/alerting, backups verified.

Estimated effort: a focused multi-sprint program. The foundation is solid; the work is wiring + hardening + responsive UI, not a rebuild.
